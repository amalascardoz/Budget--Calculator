BUDGET CALCULATOR

•The Budget Calculator evaluates the components of a personal budget and highlights which specific areas need improvement.

•A budget lets you see if you are spending more or less than you earn, can help you get on top of bills and show you how much is available to save towards future goals

•This calculator uses the 50/30/20 budget to suggest how much of your monthly income to allocate to needs, wants and savings.
